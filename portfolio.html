Featured Case Study

A closer look at one of our comprehensive projects

Integrated Digital Transformation for RetailCo

RetailCo, a mid-sized retail chain with 20+ locations, approached AXSYNTHE Group with the challenge of modernizing their entire digital ecosystem to better serve customers and streamline operations.

Our cross-company team worked together to deliver a comprehensive solution that included:

  • Dev.Ora: Responsive e-commerce website with inventory integration
  • Edvana: Customer mobile app with loyalty program and in-store navigation
  • Pixora: Product photography and promotional videos
  • Creovis: Refreshed brand identity and digital marketing assets
  • LedGrix: Implementation of integrated financial reporting system

The result was a 45% increase in online sales, 30% improvement in customer engagement, and 25% reduction in operational costs within the first year after implementation.
